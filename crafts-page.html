Fabric Doorstop

This is great item to have in all homes. Not only is it useful, but by making one yourself, you can customise it to fit your décor style.

Drawstring Bag

Reversible Bag

This craft offers double the amount of versatility and style as it can be flipped inside out to reveal another look, depending on the materials you decide to use.
(INSTRUCTIONS)

Hanging Cloth

This creation takes an everyday household and turns it into something even more practical, a cloth with a loop of fabric on one end to hang it up with.

Card Pouch
