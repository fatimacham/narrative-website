What is Sewing?

Sewing is the craft of fastening or attaching objects using stitches made with a sewing needle and thread.

Sewing is one of the oldest of the textile arts, arising in the Palaeolithic era (by Wikipedia).

The act or process of using a needle and thread to make or repair something, such as a piece of clothing (by Britannica Dictionary).
